Bio

Joji was born on September 18, 1992 in Osaka, Japan. His stage name, Joji, is actually derived from the japanese pronounciation of the name “George”, which is his real name.

Birds eye view of Osaka

Career

The genres Joji Is most known for creating music in are R+B, trip-hop, and lo-fi. All of which are relatively new genres to the world of music. He is currently signed with the record label 88rising, though in the past he was signed with 12 tone music.

Current Record Deal

Currently, Joji is signed with 88rising; a media company focused on Asian artists. Founded by Sean Miyashiro and headquartered in New York City, the mass media company is described as being a hybrid management, record label, video production, and marketing company.
